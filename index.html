Subscribe to Calendar

All Youth EU events will automatically appear in your calendar app and stay up to date.

iPhone & Mac (Apple Calendar)

  1. Tap the button below on your iPhone
  2. Tap Subscribe when prompted
  3. Done — events sync automatically
Open in Apple Calendar

Or go to Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar and paste the URL below.

Android (Google Calendar)

  1. Open Google Calendar on desktop
  2. Click + next to "Other calendars" → From URL
  3. Paste the calendar URL below and click Add calendar

Outlook (Desktop & Web)

  1. Open Outlook Calendar
  2. Click Add calendar → Subscribe from web
  3. Paste the URL above and click Import
